🔒 Your security, built in from day one
✓You're always in control. Your assistant drafts and stages messages — nothing is sent to a customer without your one-tap approval.
✓Bank-level connection. Accounts connect through Google's official secure sign-in (OAuth). Locale never sees or stores your password.
✓Only what's needed. Your assistant is given specific, limited permissions — like a trusted employee with a defined role, not the keys to everything.
✓Revoke anytime. Disconnect any account in one click, instantly cutting off all access.
✓Full audit trail. Every action your assistant takes is logged, so you can always see exactly what happened.
