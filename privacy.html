Privacy & Terms

Last updated: June 2026

Plain English, no surprises. Here's exactly what we collect, what we do with it, and the terms of using Locale. If anything's unclear, just ask.

Our privacy promise

Locale is built for local business owners, and we treat your data the way we'd want ours treated. We don't sell your information. We don't share it with advertisers. We collect only what we need to run the service and help your business, and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

The short version: your business data is yours. We use it to power your tools and your dashboard, nothing else. We never sell it, and you can export or delete it whenever you like.

What we collect

Information you give us

  • Account details — your name, email, and password (passwords are encrypted; we never see them in plain text).
  • Business information — details about your business that you enter to use the tools: your services, your jobs, your content plans, your customers' contact details that you choose to store.
  • Audit answers — the responses you give in the free visibility audit, and the email address you provide to see your results.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic usage data — which features you use, so we can improve the product. Standard, anonymous, and not tied to selling anything.

Connected accounts (only if you choose)

If you connect a third-party account — like your Google Business Profile — we access only the specific data needed to show your reviews and visibility numbers in your dashboard. We request the minimum permissions required, we never post or change anything without your action, and you can disconnect at any time, which immediately revokes our access.

How we use your information

  • To run the tools you signed up for — your Job Tracker, Content Studio, visibility score, and dashboard.
  • To send you service messages you've asked for, like follow-up reminders (which are opt-in and you control).
  • To process your subscription payment (handled securely by Stripe — we never store your card details).
  • To improve Locale based on how the product is actually used.

That's the full list. We don't use your data to build advertising profiles, and we don't feed your customers' details to anyone.

Who we share it with

Only the services that make Locale work, and only the minimum needed:

  • Stripe — to process payments securely. They handle your card; we never see it.
  • Supabase — our secure database provider, where your account data is stored.
  • Email/SMS providers — only to deliver messages you've opted into, like reminders.
  • Google — only if you connect your Google Business Profile, and only to read the data you've authorised.

We never sell your data, and we never share it for advertising. If the law ever required us to disclose something, we'd tell you unless legally prevented.

Your rights & choices

  • See your data — ask us for a copy of what we hold about you.
  • Delete it — ask us to delete your account and data, and we will.
  • Disconnect accounts — unlink any connected service in one click, cutting off our access instantly.
  • Turn off messages — reminders and check-ins are opt-in; switch them off any time in Settings.

To exercise any of these, just email us (below) and we'll sort it out.

Terms of service

The basics

By using Locale, you agree to these terms. We provide the tools described on our site, on a subscription basis, and we do our honest best to keep them running well and improving.

Your subscription

The Toolkit is a recurring monthly subscription. You can cancel any time, and you won't be charged for the following month. We don't lock you into contracts. Payments are processed securely by Stripe.

What we promise — and what we don't

We promise to provide the tools honestly and keep your data safe. We help you become more visible and follow up your leads, but — like any tool — results depend on how you use it and factors outside our control (the market, your competition, your effort). We can't and don't guarantee specific business outcomes, rankings, or revenue. Any examples or calculators on our site are illustrations, not promises.

Your responsibilities

  • Keep your login secure and don't share your account.
  • Only store customer information you're entitled to store, and handle it responsibly.
  • Use Locale lawfully and not to send spam or anything dodgy.

Fair use & changes

We may update these terms or the product as it grows; we'll let you know about anything significant. If we ever change pricing, existing subscribers get fair notice. We may suspend accounts that abuse the service, but we'll always try to talk first.
